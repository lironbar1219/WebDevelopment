Time Sqauare

  • Times Square is one of the world's busiest pedestrian areas and a major center of the world's entertainment industry.
  • The New Year's Eve ball drop at Times Square is a globally celebrated event, drawing over a million visitors annually.
  • It's renowned for its massive digital billboards, Broadway theaters, and a variety of shops and restaurants.
  • Originally named Longacre Square, it was renamed Times Square in 1904 after The New York Times moved its headquarters there.
  • Times Square has been featured in countless movies and TV
