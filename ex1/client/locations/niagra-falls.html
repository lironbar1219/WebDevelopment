Niagra Falls

  • Niagara Falls consists of three waterfalls: Horseshoe Falls, American Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls.
  • It's located on the border between the United States and Canada, offering spectacular views from both sides.
  • The falls have the highest flow rate of any waterfall in North America.
  • They are a source of hydroelectric power, contributing to the power needs of nearby areas.
  • Niagara Falls State Park is the oldest state park in the United States, established in 1885.
