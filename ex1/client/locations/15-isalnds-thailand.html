Thailand's 15 islands

  • Thailand's islands are known for their stunning natural beauty, encompassing lush rainforests and crystal-clear waters.
  • Phuket, the largest island, is famous for its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches.
  • The Phi Phi Islands are celebrated for their spectacular rock formations and vivid underwater coral gardens.
  • The Similan Islands are a top destination for scuba divers, offering some of the clearest waters in the world.
  • Koh Tao is renowned for its excellent diving opportunities and relaxed island atmosphere.
