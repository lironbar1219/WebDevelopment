Tokyo

  • Tokyo, blending ultra-modern with the traditional, is the capital city of Japan and the world's most populous metropolis.
  • It's known for its towering skyscrapers, diverse shopping districts, and an array of sushi and ramen eateries.
  • Tokyo's Shibuya Crossing is one of the busiest pedestrian crossings in the world.
  • The city is home to the historic Imperial Palace and the ancient Sensoji Temple, reflecting its rich cultural heritage.
  • Tokyo has more Michelin-starred restaurants than any other city, making it a top destination for food enthusiasts.
